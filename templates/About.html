About Movie Wala

Your simple and interactive movie tracker app

What is Movie Wala?

Movie Wala is a user-friendly web application that allows you to keep track of your favorite movies. You can add new movies, describe their genre or type, and manage your movie list efficiently with easy update and delete options.

Key Features

Add Movies

Easily add your favorite movies with their name and description/type.

Update Movies

Update movie details whenever needed to keep your list accurate and up-to-date.

Delete Movies

Remove movies from your list with a single click once they are no longer needed.

Built With

  • Python (Flask Framework)
  • SQLite Database (via SQLAlchemy)
  • HTML, CSS, Bootstrap for frontend
